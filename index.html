About Us

The story behind the counter.

Since day one

Krios started as a simple idea: a counter where fizzy drinks, milkshakes, ice cream and candy all live under one roof, made fresh in front of you instead of pulled off a shelf.

Every mocktail is shaken to order, every milkshake is blended fresh per glass, and our ice cream is churned in-house. Nothing sits around waiting — it's made when you ask for it.

Whether you're stopping by for a quick fizzy sip after class or settling in with friends over snow flacks, Krios is built to be the neighbourhood's go-to bubble stop.
